Kaleidoscope

This dance was written by Natalie Silva

Caller's Notes for Kaleidoscope

Opening:

(8) Set all around

(2) Turn partner by the Right 1/2 so that the men face out and the ladies in. Take contra corner's Left hand to make one 8-hand circle.

(2) Rise & Grind.

(2) Turn contra corner by the Left.

(2) Turn partner by the Right to place.

Body (44):

(16) Skip Across

(2) All 4 men at once: skip across passing Right shoulder. Let the man on your Left go before you.

(2) Turn opposite lady by the Left 1-1/4 around.

(4) Turn contra corner 1-1/2 by the Right to her partner's place.

(2) All 4 men at once: skip across passing Right shoulder.

(2) Turn opposite (original corner) lady by the Left 3/4 around.

(4) Turn partner by the Right.

(12) Cheese Wheel-7s right instead of left, turn corner by left, partner by right

(2) ALL dancers do 7s to their own right

(2) All set.

(2) Ladies drop partner’s hand and make a circle of four , do 7s left

Gents stay still

(2) All set.

(4) Ladies turn their corner by the Left then turn partner by the Right to place. 2 bars each.

(16) Country Squareand Reverse, Heads in first

ALWAYS FACE THE SAME DIRECTION

(2) Heads advance with their partners as Sides do 7s away from their partner

(2) Heads take opposite’s hands and do 7s away from their partner while Sides advance and take their opposite’s hands

(2) Heads retire from their opposite while Sides do 7s toward their partner while holding their opposite’s hands

(2) Heads do 7s toward their partner while Sides retire with their partner

(2) Heads do 7s away from their partner as Sides advance with their partners

(2) Heads advance and take their opposite’s hands while Sides take opposite’s hands and do 7s away from their partner

(2) Heads do 7s toward their partner while holding their opposite’s hands while Sides retire from their opposite

(2) Heads retire with their partner while Sides do 7s toward their partner

Figure 1 (16):

(8) Ring Right Top lady dances in the center

(4) Sets take hands in a circle and sidestep together (7s) and then set (3s) twice.

(4) Repeat to place.

(8) Ring Left Bottom lady dances in the center

Same as above

Body

Figure 2 (32):

(32) Iris:(Created by Laura Gorrin)

Ladies follow partner 2 counts behind, gents follow contra corner 2 counts behind.

7s to partner’s/contra corner’s place, set

Repeat until home

Body

Figure 3 (16):

(8) Ring Right First side lady dances in the center

Same as above

(8) Ring Left Second side lady dances in the center

Same as above

Body

Figure 4 (36):

(2) Turn Partner about 1-¼-Tops by Right, Sides by Left

Turn into two sets of 4

(32) Star Pull Through Chain:

(8) Full Star Pull Through Tops with sides right of them

Full right hand star

Pull through to the next couple (Should be the couple to the left of Top’s home, right of Side’s home)

(8) Star Pull Through With the new couple you’re facing

Full left hand star

Pull through to the next couple (Should be the couple to the right of your home/couple you started this figure with)

(16) Repeat

(2) Turn into place

Body

Closing:

(8) Lead Around
