Who are we?

Hi, I'm Natalie!

I made this website is to help make Irish dancing/Ceili dancing easier to teach and learn. There are still some bugs that need to be ironed out but I hope this site has been helpful!

I'm an Irish dancer from Berkeley, California, and I learned at the Starry Plough. After learning there I really fell in love with Irish Ceili and wish to spread that joy!
