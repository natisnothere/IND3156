Siege of Ennis

This dance was published by Terry Clyde F. O'Neal II

(8) Advance & Retire twice.

(8) Slipsides as couple (Double Slipsides).

Couples Slipsides in their original lines and back.

(8) Various things (varies locally).

Center 4 Star Right & Left while the ends turn their opposites by the Right & then Left.

OR

All swing opposite.

(8) Advance, Retire & Pass Through.

Those facing the music raise hands and let the other pass under. Pass by the Right shoulder.
